                                <p class="item-intro text-muted">Sunday, October 9, 2016</p>
                                <p>FASTERCON was the inaugural annual conference hosted at UC Berkeley by FASTER (Filipino Americans in Silicon Valley Tech) whose aim was to create, connect, and cultivate a valuable movement of Filipino Americans in the technology industry, with a focus on the San Francisco Bay Area. This event is co-organized by FASTER, UCB PASS (Pilipino Academic Student Services, UCB PASAE (Pilipino Association of Scientists, Architects and Engineers),and UCB PAAC (Cal Pilipino American Alumni Chapter).
This year's conference was focused on highlighting and strengthening the network of Filipino American technology professionals in the Bay Area and included several keynote speeches on the future of Filipino Americans in tech, workshops on entrepreneurship and design, and panel discussions on engineering and professional development for underrepresented communities in the industry.</p>
